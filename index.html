Highlights Of Package

  • Enjoy the Kecak fire dance performance at Uluwatu Temple, where traditional dance and dramatic sunset create an unforgettable cultural experience.
  • Experience the romance of Bali, where sunset strolls along pristine beaches, dinners under the stars, and amazing moments in lush rice terraces create unforgettable memories.
  • Indulge in a Balinese massage experience, where rhythmic strokes, acupressure, and aromatic oils come together to offer a deeply relaxing experience.
  • Set sail on a thrilling Pirate Dinner Cruise, where lively pirate-themed entertainment, delicious cuisine, and stunning ocean views create an unforgettable evening.
  • Explore the stunning Tegallalang Rice Terraces, where tiered landscapes of lush greenery create a serene atmosphere and offer a glimpse into Bali’s traditional farming culture.
  • Yacht party complimentary with our package.
  • Offbeat locations such as Dolphin Watching, Lahangan Sweet, Kanto Lampo Waterfall etc.
